About Mitra Path

We believe that poverty is the root cause of crime and many other social problems we face today. And we know that the most effective way to deal with poverty is to create work through small businesses.

However, it isn’t enough that work is created. The work must be meaningful.
It should enable us to:

  • Meet one or more needs of our society
  • Earn our livelihood, and
  • Discover who we are and our potential

Work requires our care and nurturing before we can benefit from the work in a sustained way. For this, we need to practice mindfulness. Mindfulness is a way to develop the ability to be aware of what’s happening now without judgment.

Our Team

President

Currently CEO and President of Calsoft Systems, an IT consulting company Torrance, California.
